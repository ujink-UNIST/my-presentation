Critical Review

What was demonstrated

  • Inverse design using the full stress–strain curve as the target.
  • Bidirectional structure–response representation.
  • Large automated FE dataset and customized response examples.

What remains limited

  • The design variable is a 28D binary strut on/off vector.
  • Struts are idealized with uniform cross-sections; radius, smoothness, curved struts, and joint shape are not explicit design variables.
  • Out-of-distribution generalization and one-to-many diversity remain unclear.
