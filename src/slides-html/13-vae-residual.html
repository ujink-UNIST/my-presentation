Multi-Task VAE with Residual Predictor

Why VAE + residual predictor?

  • VAE branch: learns a compact latent space for reconstructing discrete lattice topologies.
  • Prediction branch: maps the latent representation to the nonlinear stress–strain curve.
  • Residual module: corrects curve details that the main predictor misses, especially peaks and oscillatory regions.
