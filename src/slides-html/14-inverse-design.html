Paper's inverse design procedure

Database-assisted inverse design

  • Target response is represented by curve features such as peak locations and amplitudes.
  • Similar structures are retrieved from the trained/database space.
  • Bar-number and connectivity constraints filter infeasible candidates.
  • FE simulation verifies whether the selected topology follows the target curve.
Review point: generated designs are convincing, but novelty and one-to-many diversity depend on database coverage.
